Understanding the Environmental Impact Profile

This radar chart illustrates the environmental impact of {{ pesticide }} across six key dimensions. Each axis represents a different aspect, rated from 0 (low impact) to 10 (high impact):

  • Soil Mobility: How easily the pesticide moves through soil.
  • Aquatic Toxicity: Potential harm to aquatic life.
  • Leaching Potential: Likelihood of contaminating groundwater.
  • Volatility: Tendency to vaporize and affect air quality.
  • Bee Toxicity: Potential harm to bee populations.
  • Bird/Mammal Toxicity: Average potential harm to birds and mammals.

A larger area on the chart indicates higher overall environmental impact. Consider these factors when planning pesticide use and implement appropriate mitigation strategies.
